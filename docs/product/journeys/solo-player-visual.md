## Summary

- **Persona:** Casual-Idle-Time Player
- **Goal:** Complete a game vs AI in <90s with zero setup
- **Platform:** Web
- **Emotional arc:** Curious → Focused → Satisfied

## Step outline

1. Arrive & See Board
2. Human Makes a Move
3. AI Responds
4. Game Ends (Win/Loss/Draw)
5. Play Again or Leave
